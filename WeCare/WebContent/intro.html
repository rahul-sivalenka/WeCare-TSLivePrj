Cancer Awareness for Women

Objective

Spread awareness about the various and most harmful cancers which can affect women. A platform for medical practitioners and social service organizations to engage in community awareness and education programs. Knowledge of measures that can be taken to prevent these life threatening diseases can lead to the enormous decrease in the number of victims. Early diagnosis and treatment can help cure the disease easily.

Users of the system

Multiple profiles of users shall operate this system.

  1. Administrator
  2. Doctors and Medical Activists
  3. Woman Welfare Organizations
  4. Community User

High Level Requirements

  1. Medical practitioners should be able to upload write-ups which give a general awareness to the public about the hazardous cancers which can affect women.
  2. System to keep track of the activities undertaken like - counselling, participation in camps, write-ups uploaded by the Doctor and Medical Activist.
  3. The most active doctor or medical practitioner in each disease category should be recognized.
  4. Woman Welfare Organizations should be able to announce the launch of programs or diagnosis camps through the site.
  5. System be able to suggest programs or diagnosis camps near to the user's residence and users be able to register for various programs online.
  6. Be able to get various reports like program , diagnosis camp wise registration
  7. Be able to upload program , diagnosis camp related data
  8. User should be able to create, update profile though not mandatory. Their details should be disclosed only if they wish to do so
  9. Affected or cured women should be able to share their experiences through the site.
  10. Users should be able to attend Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) to help identify whether they have started to show the initial symptoms.
  11. System be able to show a list of practitioners in the vicinity of the user.
  12. Be able to clarify their doubts about the disease with medical activists and Doctors.
  13. System should send a regular newsletter registered users about the latest developments in regard to the disease.
